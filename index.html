VALiChain

Validated Ownership on the Blockchain

KYC-verified digital ownership ledger that makes physical assets verifiable, transferable, and fraud-proof. From luxury goods to government titles.

The Problem

Premium goods markets and government registries face critical challenges that cost billions annually

$30B+ Lost to Counterfeits

Luxury brands lose massive revenue to counterfeit goods. Paper certificates and digital scans are easily forged, making authenticity verification nearly impossible.

Fragmented Records

Ownership data is scattered across receipts, databases, and unverifiable certificates. Government registries are often local, paper-based, and siloed.

Slow & Costly Transfers

Asset transfers take weeks, involve multiple parties, and incur high fees. Vehicle transfers require DMV visits with 10-30 day delays.

The Solution

VALiChain creates digital twins for physical assets, enabling instant, verifiable, and compliant ownership transfers

Hardware-Bound Digital Twins

Each physical asset is paired with an NFT bound to NFC/secure-element chips, creating tamper-proof provenance records.

KYC-Verified Transfers

Only verified individuals can hold or transfer ownership, ensuring regulatory compliance and reducing fraud.

Verification Marketplace

Accredited authenticators verify products on-demand and receive automated commission payouts through smart contracts.
